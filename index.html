WHAT IT IS

Pledge 1% San Diego is an opportunity for companies of all sizes to leverage a portion of your time, talent, and/or treasure to support public education in San Diego County.

1%

Equity

Pledging 1% of equity can be one of the most simple, effective, and impactful ways to give as a company.

1%

Time

The best way to start giving back as a company of any size or stage. 1% of a 40-hour week is just 20 minutes!

1%

Product

Share your product with growing non-profits, educational organizations, or those most in need. 

1%

Profit

We recognize that companies are all at different stages and have different levels of giving.

WHAT IT MEANS FOR STUDENTS

The Pledge 1% campaign will allow greater support to underserved schools through financial support, classroom products, and volunteers for work-based learning.

HOW IT WORKS

You make the pledge, we help you implement. No matter what pledge you choose for your organization, we strive to make the process as easy as possible.

1

Choose a pledge type

Choose a pledge type that is best for your company

2

Submit the pledge form

Take the pledge on our site by clicking on this form.                         

3

Meet with us

Meet with us to learn more about your company and we will follow-up with the most relevant resources based on your responses

4

Act, participate and give back

Who you give to, when you give, and how much is completely up to you! We encourage companies to give back as much as they can; however, it’s important that you do whatever makes sense for your company’s long-term growth and business model

5

Share your experience

Share your experience and best practices with our network, receiving extra exposure and recognition as a leader of the movement

If you are at an early stage, finalize your company equity donation as soon as possible. The sooner, the better from an ease of execution standpoint!

If you are a late-stage company, we recommend connecting with existing companies who have gone through this process to learn from their experience. We would be happy to intro you to other Pledge 1% members who have gone through this process at a similar stage and who can guide you on next steps.

No matter your company’s stage or size, take the pledge as the first step today.

Many companies start by giving time and commit to new Pledge Types when they feel they are ready to do more. Although giving time may be the easiest way to start giving (1% of a 40 hour week is just 20 minutes!), it’s important to develop a program that best fits with your company’s culture and larger goals.

Time donations can come in many shapes and sizes—if you are interested in giving time, join Pledge 1% to receive more resources and to set up a time to talk with our team about the best approach for you.

We have a great guide and track record on helping companies set up their product donations. Join Pledge 1% now to learn more and get one-on-one guidance to support your program development.

It’s important to think about the type of company you have when you are deciding how you will give profit. If you are a LLC or S-Corp, giving 1% of profit is easier and more beneficial than giving 1% of equity. When you join Pledge 1%, we’ll provide you with the appropriate guidance and paperwork for you to set up your profit donation program quickly and easily. Take the pledge to get started today!

ABOUT US

Pledge 1% San Diego is the regional initiative of the global Pledge 1% movement, which catalyzes startups and other innovation economy players to make supporting their communities integral to what they do. By pledging a small portion of their future success today, companies can have an enormous impact.

Pledge 1% San Diego is an initiative powered by both the San Diego County Office of Education's Innovation Division and the Classroom of the Future Foundation
